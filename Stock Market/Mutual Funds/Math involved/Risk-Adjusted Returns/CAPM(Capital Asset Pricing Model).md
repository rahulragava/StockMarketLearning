## ðŸ”¹ Formula

$$

Where:  
- **\(E(R_i)\)** = Expected return of stock/fund *i*  
- **\(R_f\)** = Risk-free rate (e.g., government bond return)

- **\((R_m - R_f)\)** = Market Risk Premium (extra reward for taking risk beyond risk-free asset)
